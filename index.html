Our Story

From struggle to steam

Hi, I’m Hafsa. Caffeine Cove started as scribbles in a notebook between lectures and a weekend stall outside campus. Money was tight. I repaired a second-hand grinder with YouTube tutorials, practiced latte art on milk cartons, and wrote every expense on tiny chits that still live in our cash box.

Bank loans said “not yet.” Suppliers asked for deposits I couldn’t meet. So I started with what I had—a borrowed kettle, a single-origin batch roasted by a friend, and a promise to never compromise on taste. I failed, adjusted, failed smarter. Slowly, regulars became friends. Friends became family.

Today, Caffeine Cove is our little cove at TSC More, Dhaka—a warm corner for students pulling an all-nighter, couples on a rainy date, and anyone needing a quiet minute with a good cup. We still taste every shot. We still write thank-you notes. And yes—we still celebrate the tiny wins (like nailing a swan five days in a row).

If you’re reading this, please come say hi. Sit near the window. Tell us your name. We’ll remember it on your next visit.
